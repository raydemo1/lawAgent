# Prioritize Material-Driven Review in Phase 2

LawAgent Phase 2 prioritizes material-driven compliance review over standalone legal Q&A. Standalone regulation research remains as the no-material fallback, but the main product loop starts from a user's business material or scenario, extracts review facts, retrieves applicable legal evidence, checks evidence sufficiency, and returns risk level, missing information, recommended actions, and citations.

This keeps the product aligned with its primary user: enterprise data compliance reviewers who need a traceable judgment on a concrete processing activity, not a generic legal search box.
